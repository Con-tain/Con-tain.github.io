About Us

With over 40 years experience in the Nuclear Sector we at Raddecom Solutions are providing novel designs for complex decommissioning problems with cost effective, robust, flexible, sustainable products. We work with manufacturers supporting the nuclear sector to provide differentiated products that showcase UK manufacturing.

Since 2015 we have been developing a range of precast concrete containers for the interim storage, transport and final disposal of radioactive wastes. Take a look at some of the containers that make up our Con-tain Range!
