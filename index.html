Welcome to Con-tain

UK and overseas markets are seeking low cost, safe packaging solutions for radioactive decommissioning wastes. At RADDECOM SOLUTIONS we are providing our customers with bespoke and novel solutions that deliver a range of benefits and cost savings that other market suppliers are not able to match.

With the success of our precast concrete waste containers, the "CON-TAIN RANGE", we now have over 40 designs registered with the UK Intellectual Property Office (IPO), from simple concrete containers to containers designed for remote handling with a number of these designs adopted by waste consigners.

Whether you want to use one of our existing concrete container designs or need a bespoke solution then we can deliver a product that will meet your needs.
